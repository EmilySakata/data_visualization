below visualization graphs are used to perform comparison to obtain meaningful insight and analysis of runners from the 2017 San Francisco Marathon. The data from official race results were compared with data from Strava users to understand the demographics of Strava users compared to the overall runners, distribution of the Strava users final results, and the average pace analysis of the top10 runners.
